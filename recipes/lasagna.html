The best lasagna you've ever tasted

This lasagna recipe is a family favorite, passed down through generations. It's rich, hearty, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef, onion, and garlic until well browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine mozzarella cheese, ricotta cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. To assemble, spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer with 4 noodles, then spread with one-third of the cheese mixture. Repeat layers twice more, ending with meat sauce on top.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes. Let stand for 15 minutes before serving.
