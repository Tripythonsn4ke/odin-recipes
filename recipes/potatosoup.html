Hearty and comforting potato soup

This potato soup recipe is perfect for a cozy meal. It's creamy, filling, and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds russet or Yukon potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and garlic; saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add cubed potatoes and chicken broth. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
  5. Using an immersion blender or regular blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  6. Add heavy cream and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 5 more minutes.
  7. Garnish with chopped chives before serving.
